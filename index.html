STEP  2 

Fill the Sand Pouring Cylinder (SPC) with sand and find its weight, (W1).

STEP  3 

Allow the sand of volume equal to that of the calibrating container to run out of cylinder by opening the shutter. Measure weight of calibrating cantainer with sand without sand.

STEP  4 

Place the SPC on a glass plate, open the slit above the cone by operating the value and allow the sand to run down. The sand will freely run down till it fills the conical portion and note down the weight of sand in the conical portion.

STEP  5 

Clean and level the soil to be tested. Then Keep the metal tray on the level surface and excavate a circular hole of volume equal to that of calibrating container. Measure weight of excavated soil in the tray and weight of sand pouring cylinder with sand after pouring in to the hole.

STEP  6 

Determination of water content.

STEP  7
